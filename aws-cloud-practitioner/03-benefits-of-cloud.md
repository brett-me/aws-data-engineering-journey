# benefits of cloud

- agility
- pay as you go pricing
- economy of scale
- global reach
- security
- reliability
- high availability
- elasticity
- fault tolerance
- disaster recovery

## six advantages to cloud (original description)

### 1. trade capital expense for variable expense

- pay on demand
- no upfront cost

### 2. massive economies of scale

- share costs with other customers

### 3. stop guessing capacity

- scale up or down the meet current need
- launch or destroy services

### 4. speed and agility

- launch resources within a few clicks

### 5. stop money spent on running and maintaining data centers

- focus on own customers / primary services

### 6. go global in minutes

- deploy app in multiple regions with a few clicks
- lower latency and better experience for customers
